Shenzhen Huawanda Electronic Co., Ltd. was established in 2008. we are a company which can research, develop, produce and sell USB flash drive, power bank, mobile phone accessories and other digital products.
Our price advantage, quality assurance and intact service system make us excellent in our field. We provide OEM and ODM services for many well-known international and domestic brands, Moreover, we win favor and praises from many foreign markets for instance Western Europe,America and Mid East.
At present, our company has a factory which occupies 10,000 square meters and employs more than 1000 staff members. Our company also has a number of production lines with the most advanced patch assembly technology. Furthermore, we have automatic plug procedure wave soldering equipment, wave soldering machines, and a whole set of automatic testing equipment.
our enterprise spirit is "unity, fighting and realistic, innovation" and we can keep the business philosophy of "Quality First, Customer First" in our mind forever. Taking advantage of our capabilities, we can create a better future.
We are looking forward to establishing cooperative relationships with you. Please contact us for more information.
